How to Make an Origami Penguin

This easy origami penguin is perfect for beginners. All you need is a square piece of paper!

What You Need:

  • 1 Square of Origami Paper (Black and white is best!)
  • Your hands
  • 5 minutes of time

Step 1: The Base Fold

Start with the colored side down. Fold the paper in half diagonally to create a triangle, then unfold.

Step 2: Fold the Sides

Fold the two side corners in to meet the center crease you just made.

Step 3: Creating the Head

Fold the top point down to create the beak and head of your penguin.

Step 4: The Final Shape

Fold the entire model in half and pull the head forward slightly. Your penguin is done!
