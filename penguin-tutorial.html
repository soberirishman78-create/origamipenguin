Step-by-Step Penguin Tutorial

Follow these simple steps to create your own paper penguin mascot.

Step 1: The Base Fold

Start with a square piece of paper. Fold it in half diagonally to create a triangle, then unfold.

Step 2: Create the Body

Fold the two side corners into the center crease to form a kite shape.

Step 3: The Head and Beak

Fold the top point down to create the head, then fold a small part of that point back up to form the beak.

Step 4: The Feet

Fold the bottom corner up slightly to create a flat base for the penguin to stand on.

Congratulations! You've made your first origami penguin!
